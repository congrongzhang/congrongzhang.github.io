Congrong Zhang

Beijing, China · congrong.zhang@uq.edu.au

I hold a Master of Interaction from the University of Queensland, where my thesis focused on employing participatory design methods to foster human-AI interaction within the healthcare filed. My research interests center on exploring how emerging technologies, particularly AI, can be leveraged to design and organize workshop materials and processes that enhance collaboration and connectivity across diverse professional fields.
